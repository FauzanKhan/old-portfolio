A few Words from the Director

Free Spirits is an initiative to a greener world. It aims to innovate products that helps people to save their money as well as preserving the conventional sources of energy. A company that encourages the use of products that helps saving electricity and also eliminates the use of non-renewable sources of energy.
A huge thanks to Prof. Kumar Krishen for being a guide and a source of inspiration for us.
We share a common dream and are on the path of fulfilling it together.We are a group of young engineers who are dedicated to to reduce the gap between technology and you so that one day you could light up your homes for free.

A heart touching story during one of our projects

In one of the houses where we have installed the solar setup, lives a widow with her 3 children. They didn't have any electricity privilege ever. There was no way to work at nights except for the kerosene lamps. The kids also used to study under the kerosene lamp. Later, after dusk, on a regular check up to test the proper functioning of the system installed, we saw the kids studying in the lamp lightened by solar power. The happiness and the feeling of that family cannot be expressed in words but we would like to relive that happiness again and again.

About Us

free Spirits is one company that does not believe in developing products and selling them to the market. We have a different view of thinking. We are a group of passionate people who share a common objective, and that is to develop new and amazing applications of renewable energy.
We are dedicated to remove the gap between technology and common people. We develop products that are strong, rugged and easy to repair.
free Spirits offers the installation of solar plants utilising Photovoltaic power (PV) in India. Project Rashmi

August 25, 2013

A small village in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh electricity is like a distant dream for the inhabitants. We inquired about the problems faced by the villagers on a daily basis. On interviewing some of them, one of the problems was considered and then concluded that they pay Rs. 5 every alternate day to charge their mobile phones. At times they wait for hours for their turn. The monthly cost of charging their mobile phones approximates around Rs. 100 â€“ 120. To address this problem, we have come up with Project RASHMI, which aims to provide solar mobile phone chargers to the villagers. The advantage being the phone is charged during the day without paying any rent to the owner of the station and also the long queues would be rejected .Every villager would have their personal charging station with them. The monthly expenditure would drop to Rs. 19 for mobile phone charging.

Electricity Saver Device

July, 2013

A PIR based motion sensor device which detects the motion of body heat and allows the device to turn ON/OFF accordingly. Once the motion is detected, the appliance is turned ON for an adjustable delay time. If the motion is continued to be detected, the delay time extends.
For details about installing this device in your home, office or garden and to get a quote, email your details to support@freespirits.in
